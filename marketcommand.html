About the App

MarketCommand is the all-in-one platform for street fairs, farmers markets, and county fairs — connecting organizers and vendors with intelligent booth mapping, automated document collection, and streamlined event management.

Built for the cities, organizers, and small businesses that make markets happen, MarketCommand turns chaotic event days into fast, compliant, revenue-positive operations that scale effortlessly.
